Project Details

My Contributions

  • Core player movement system (grid locked NES behavior)
  • Enemy implementation (Keese, Stalfos, Aquamentus boss, Goriya)
  • Enemy spawning
  • Combat systems (Bomb)
  • Room transitions, old man room, and door locking logic
  • Custom mechanics (Frost Boots logic)

Technology Used

  • Unity (WebGL build)
  • C#

Controls

  • 'WASD' or arrow key inputs for movement
  • 'Z' key is to use your primary weapon slot
  • 'X' key is to use your secondary weapon slot
  • Space key cycles your secondary weapon
  • '1' key activates cheats, giving unlimited health and infinite keys, bombs, and rupees
  • '4' key is a shortcut to play the custom room added to the dungeon (After defeating Aquamentus)
